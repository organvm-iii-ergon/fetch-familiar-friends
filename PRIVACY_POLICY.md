## Introduction

1. **Local-First**: All your data stays on your device by default
2. **No Tracking**: We don't track you or sell your data
3. **User Control**: You own your data and can export or delete it anytime
4. **Transparency**: We're clear about what we collect and why

---

## Data We Collect and Store Locally

All of the following data is stored **locally on your device** in your browser's localStorage. We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

### 1. Journal Entries
- **What**: Your daily journal entries about your pet
- **Why**: To help you track memories and reflections
- **Storage**: Browser localStorage on your device
- **Access**: Only you, through your browser
- **Retention**: Until you delete it or clear browser data

### 2. Favorite Images
- **What**: URLs of dog/cat images you've marked as favorites
- **Why**: To let you build a collection of images you love
- **Storage**: Browser localStorage (URLs only, not the images themselves)
- **Access**: Only you
- **Retention**: Until you delete them or clear browser data

### 3. Settings and Preferences
- **What**: Your theme choice, animation preferences, notification settings
- **Why**: To personalize your experience
- **Storage**: Browser localStorage
- **Access**: Only you
- **Retention**: Until you change them or clear browser data

### 4. Error Logs (Local Only)
- **What**: Technical error information if the app crashes
- **Why**: To help diagnose and fix problems
- **Storage**: Browser localStorage (never transmitted)
- **Contents**: Error messages, browser type (not full user agent), date (not timestamp)
- **Access**: Only you (viewable in Settings ‚Üí Advanced)
- **Retention**: Last 10 errors only, automatically pruned

## Third-Party Services

While we don't collect your data, the app uses external services to provide features:

### Dog Image API (dog.ceo)
- **Purpose**: Provides random dog images
- **Data Shared**: Your IP address (automatically sent with any web request)
- **Their Policy**: https://dog.ceo/dog-api/
- **Control**: Requests only made when you use the app

### Cat Image API (thecatapi.com)
- **Purpose**: Provides random cat images (when you toggle to cat mode)
- **Data Shared**: Your IP address
- **Their Policy**: https://thecatapi.com/privacy
- **Control**: Requests only made when you use cat mode

**Note**: When you request an image from these services, they may see your IP address as part of standard internet protocols. We don't control their data practices. Please review their privacy policies.

## Your Rights and Controls

### Right to Access
You can view all data the app stores:
1. Open Settings ‚Üí Data Management
2. Click "View Stored Data"
3. See everything we've saved locally

### Right to Export
You can export all your data:
1. Open Settings ‚Üí Data Management
2. Click "Export All Data"
3. Download a JSON file with all your information

### Right to Delete
You can delete all or some of your data:
- **Delete Specific Items**: Remove individual journal entries or favorites
- **Delete All Data**: Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí "Clear All Data"
- **Browser Clear**: Use your browser's "Clear Site Data" feature

**Important**: Once deleted, data cannot be recovered. Make sure to export before deleting if you want to keep a copy.

## Data Security

### Local Storage Security
- **Encryption**: By default, localStorage is not encrypted. If you store sensitive information in your journal, consider using the browser's private/incognito mode or enabling device encryption.
- **Browser Security**: Your data security depends on your browser and device security. Keep them updated and use strong passwords.
- **Shared Devices**: Be cautious on shared computers. Use private browsing or remember to log out.

### Optional Encryption (Future Feature)
We plan to add optional journal encryption in a future update, allowing you to password-protect sensitive entries.
